# Contact and Escalation Policy

## Support Channels

### Email Support
Send a message to support@taskflow.io. Include your workspace name and a description of your issue. Attach screenshots if relevant.

### In-App Chat
Click the "?" icon in the bottom-right corner of the web app to open the support chat. Available on Pro and Business plans during support hours.

### Community Forum
Visit community.taskflow.io for peer-to-peer support, feature discussions, and tips. TaskFlow team members actively participate in the forum. The forum is accessible to all plans including Free.

## Support Hours

- **Business days:** Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM IST (Indian Standard Time)
- **Weekends and Indian public holidays:** No live support. Emails received on weekends are responded to on the next business day.
- **Emergency support for Business plan:** Available 24/7 for workspace-wide outages. Call the emergency support line provided in your Business plan welcome email.

## Response Time SLAs by Plan

| Plan | First Response Time | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|
| Free | Best effort (community forum) | No guaranteed resolution time |
| Pro | Within 24 hours on business days | Within 3 business days |
| Business | Within 4 hours on business days | Within 1 business day |
| Enterprise | Within 1 hour (24/7) | Within 4 hours |

Response times are measured from the moment the support ticket is created. Resolution targets are goals, not guarantees — complex issues may take longer.

## Escalation Process

If you're unsatisfied with the support you've received:

1. **Level 1 — Support Agent:** Your initial support ticket is handled by a support agent. They handle common issues like account access, billing questions, and feature guidance.
2. **Level 2 — Senior Support:** If the issue isn't resolved within the resolution target, or if you request escalation, the ticket moves to a senior support engineer. Request escalation by replying to your support email with "Please escalate."
3. **Level 3 — Engineering Team:** For confirmed bugs, data issues, or technical problems that senior support cannot resolve, the ticket is escalated to the engineering team. You'll receive status updates every 24 hours until the issue is resolved.
4. **Level 4 — Management:** If you're still unsatisfied after Level 3, email escalation@taskflow.io. A support manager will review your case within 1 business day.

## Bug Reports

Report bugs through any support channel. To help us investigate faster, include:

- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Browser name and version (or mobile app version)
- Screenshots or screen recordings
- The URL you were on when the issue occurred
- Your workspace name and the project/task affected

Bug fixes are prioritized by severity:
- **Critical** (workspace inaccessible, data loss): Hotfix within 24 hours.
- **High** (feature broken for multiple users): Fix in next release (weekly release cycle).
- **Medium** (feature broken for specific cases): Fix within 2 releases.
- **Low** (cosmetic, minor inconvenience): Queued for future release.
