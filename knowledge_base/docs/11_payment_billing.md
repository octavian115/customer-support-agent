# Payment Methods and Billing FAQ

## Accepted Payment Methods

TaskFlow accepts the following payment methods:

- **Credit Cards:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Rupay
- **Debit Cards:** Visa and Mastercard debit cards
- **UPI:** Available for Indian users (monthly plans only; annual plans require card payment)
- **Net Banking:** Available for Indian users via select banks

We do not accept PayPal, wire transfers, or cryptocurrency at this time. Enterprise customers can arrange invoice-based billing — contact sales@taskflow.io.

## Billing Cycle

- **Monthly plans** are billed on the same date each month. If you subscribed on January 15, you'll be billed on the 15th of every month. If the billing date doesn't exist in a month (e.g., February 30), you'll be billed on the last day of that month.
- **Annual plans** are billed once per year on the subscription anniversary date.

All prices are in Indian Rupees (INR). GST (18%) is applied on top of the listed price for Indian customers. Invoices include GST breakdown.

## Invoices

Invoices are automatically generated for every payment and available in Workspace Settings → Billing → Invoice History. Each invoice includes:

- Workspace name and billing address
- Plan name and billing period
- Amount charged (with GST breakdown for Indian customers)
- Payment method used
- Invoice number (for accounting purposes)

Download invoices as PDF from the billing page. If you need a custom billing address or GST number on invoices, update it in Billing Settings before the next billing cycle.

## Failed Payments

If a scheduled payment fails (insufficient funds, expired card, bank decline):

1. TaskFlow retries the payment after 24 hours.
2. If the second attempt fails, TaskFlow retries again after 72 hours.
3. If the third attempt fails, your workspace is downgraded to the Free plan automatically.
4. You receive email notifications at each stage.
5. All your data is preserved. Upgrade again to restore your plan and features.

To update your payment method, go to Workspace Settings → Billing → Payment Method → Update.

## Cancelling Your Subscription

To cancel:
1. Go to Workspace Settings → Billing → Subscription.
2. Click "Cancel Subscription."
3. Select a cancellation reason (optional).
4. Confirm.

After cancellation:
- Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period.
- You are not charged again.
- Your workspace automatically switches to the Free plan when the period ends.
- All your data (projects, tasks, comments, files) is preserved, subject to Free plan limits.
