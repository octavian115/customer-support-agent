# TaskFlow Mobile App

TaskFlow is available on iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play Store.

## Supported Versions

- iOS 15.0 or later
- Android 11 or later

Older OS versions may work but are not officially supported and will not receive bug fixes.

## Setting Up the Mobile App

1. Download "TaskFlow" from your device's app store.
2. Sign in with the same email and password used for the web app, or use Google/GitHub SSO.
3. Your workspaces, projects, and tasks sync automatically.

## Mobile Features

The mobile app supports most features available on the web:

- View and edit tasks, comments, and checklists.
- Create new tasks and assign team members.
- Drag and drop tasks between lists (long-press to pick up a card).
- Upload photos and files directly from your device.
- Receive push notifications for assignments, comments, due dates, and mentions.

**Not available on mobile:**
- Timeline (Gantt) view
- Integration setup (must be configured on web)
- Workspace billing and admin settings
- Board templates

## Offline Mode

TaskFlow mobile supports offline access on Pro and Business plans:

- Tasks, projects, and boards viewed in the last 7 days are cached locally.
- You can view and edit cached tasks while offline.
- Changes sync automatically when your device reconnects to the internet.
- If there's a conflict (someone else edited the same task while you were offline), TaskFlow shows both versions and lets you choose which to keep.

Offline mode is not available on the Free plan.

## Push Notifications

By default, the mobile app sends push notifications for:

- Tasks assigned to you
- Comments on tasks you're assigned to or watching
- Due date reminders (24 hours before and at due time)
- @mentions in comments

Customize notification preferences in the mobile app under Settings → Notifications. You can mute specific projects or set Do Not Disturb hours.

## Syncing Issues

If tasks appear out of date on mobile:

1. Pull down on any screen to force a manual sync.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Ensure you're running the latest app version.
4. If the problem persists, log out and log back in — this forces a full resync.
5. Contact support if syncing issues continue after these steps.
