# TaskFlow Pricing Plans

TaskFlow offers three plans to fit teams of every size. All plans include core project management features. Upgrade or downgrade at any time.

## Free Plan

**Price:** ₹0 / forever

- Up to 5 workspace members
- Unlimited projects and tasks
- Board, List, and Calendar views
- 100 MB file storage per workspace
- 20 lists per board maximum
- 1 integration (trial)
- Community support (forum only, no guaranteed response time)
- No offline mobile access
- No Timeline view
- No webhook API

## Pro Plan

**Price:** ₹499 per month (billed monthly) or ₹4,799 per year (billed annually — save 20%)

- Unlimited workspace members
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Timeline (Gantt) view
- Unlimited lists per board
- 10 GB file storage per workspace
- Unlimited integrations (Slack, Google Calendar, GitHub)
- Offline mobile access
- Advanced search filters and saved filters
- Priority email support (response within 24 hours on business days)
- CSV import/export
- Custom labels and fields

Per-member pricing does not apply. The ₹499/month flat rate covers your entire workspace regardless of team size.

## Business Plan

**Price:** ₹1,499 per month (billed monthly) or ₹14,399 per year (billed annually — save 20%)

- Everything in Pro, plus:
- 100 GB file storage per workspace
- Admin controls (restrict member permissions, enforce 2FA, manage SSO)
- SSO (SAML 2.0) support
- Workspace analytics dashboard (task completion rates, member activity, project progress)
- Webhook API access
- Audit log (90-day history of all workspace actions)
- Priority support (response within 4 hours on business days)
- Dedicated account manager (for workspaces with 50+ members)
- Custom onboarding session for new teams

## Enterprise

For organizations with 200+ members or specific compliance needs (SOC 2, HIPAA, data residency), contact sales@taskflow.io for a custom Enterprise quote. Enterprise includes everything in Business plus custom SLAs, dedicated infrastructure, and on-premise deployment options.
