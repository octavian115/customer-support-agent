# Plan Changes: Upgrading and Downgrading

## Upgrading Your Plan

You can upgrade from Free to Pro, Free to Business, or Pro to Business at any time.

**How to upgrade:**
1. Go to Workspace Settings → Billing → Subscription.
2. Click "Change Plan."
3. Select your desired plan and billing cycle (monthly or annual).
4. Enter payment details (if not already on file).
5. Confirm the upgrade.

**When does the upgrade take effect?**
Immediately. All new features become available the moment the upgrade is confirmed. Your billing cycle starts on the upgrade date.

**Prorated charges for mid-cycle upgrades:**
If you upgrade from Pro to Business mid-cycle, you're charged only the prorated difference for the remaining days in your current billing period. For example, if you upgrade on day 15 of a 30-day cycle, you pay 50% of the price difference.

## Downgrading Your Plan

You can downgrade from Business to Pro, Business to Free, or Pro to Free.

**How to downgrade:**
1. Go to Workspace Settings → Billing → Subscription.
2. Click "Change Plan."
3. Select the lower plan.
4. Review the feature comparison to understand what you'll lose.
5. Confirm the downgrade.

**When does the downgrade take effect?**
At the end of your current billing period. You keep all current plan features until then.

**What happens to your data on downgrade?**

- **Members:** If you downgrade to Free (5-member limit) and have more than 5 members, you must remove members before the downgrade takes effect. If you don't, the 5 most recently active members (including all Admins) will retain access, and others will be suspended. Suspended members can be reinstated if you upgrade again.
- **Storage:** If your files exceed the new plan's storage limit, you won't lose any files. However, you won't be able to upload new files until you're under the limit. Download and delete files to free up space.
- **Integrations:** On downgrade to Free, all integrations except one (your most recently connected) will be disconnected. Reconnect them by upgrading again.
- **Timeline view:** Any Timeline views will become read-only on Free plan. Tasks and dependencies are preserved — you just can't edit them in Timeline view.
- **SSO:** If you downgrade from Business, SSO will be disabled. Members will need to set a password for email/password login. They'll be prompted to do this on their next login.

## Switching Billing Cycles

You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time:

- **Monthly to Annual:** You're charged the annual rate immediately, prorated for any remaining days on your current monthly cycle. The savings (20%) apply going forward.
- **Annual to Monthly:** The switch takes effect at the end of your current annual period. You cannot switch mid-year to get a partial annual refund.
