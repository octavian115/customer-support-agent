# Account Deletion

## Before You Delete

Account deletion is permanent. Before proceeding, consider these alternatives:

- **If you want to stop paying:** Cancel your subscription instead. Your workspace downgrades to Free, and all data is preserved. You can upgrade again later.
- **If you want to leave a workspace:** Go to Workspace Settings → Members → find yourself → click "Leave Workspace." Your personal account remains active.
- **If you want to remove your data from a workspace:** Ask the workspace Admin to remove you as a member. Your assigned tasks will be unassigned, but the tasks themselves remain.

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account:

- Your profile, email, and login credentials are permanently removed.
- You are removed from all workspaces you belong to.
- Tasks assigned to you become unassigned.
- Comments you posted remain in the workspace but are attributed to "Deleted User."
- Files you uploaded remain in the workspace.
- Any workspace where you are the sole Admin will also be deleted entirely (see below).

## Sole Admin Warning

If you are the only Admin in a workspace, deleting your account will delete the entire workspace and all its data — projects, tasks, files, comments, and member list. This action affects all members of that workspace.

Before deleting, either:
- Promote another member to Admin (Workspace Settings → Members → select member → Change Role to Admin).
- Or accept that the workspace and all its data will be deleted.

## How to Delete Your Account

1. Go to Account Settings → Account → Delete Account.
2. Enter your password to confirm.
3. If you have active paid subscriptions, you must cancel them first. The system will prompt you to do so.
4. Read the summary of what will be deleted.
5. Type "DELETE" in the confirmation field and click the button.

## 30-Day Recovery Window

After deletion, your account enters a 30-day recovery period:

- Your account is deactivated but not yet permanently erased.
- To recover, email recovery@taskflow.io from the email address associated with the deleted account within 30 days.
- The recovery team will reactivate your account within 1 business day.
- All your data, workspace memberships, and settings are restored as they were at the time of deletion.

After 30 days, all data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from TaskFlow's servers, including backups. We cannot recover accounts after this window.

## Exporting Your Data Before Deletion

We recommend exporting your data before deleting your account:

1. Go to Account Settings → Account → Export My Data.
2. Click "Request Export."
3. TaskFlow compiles a ZIP file containing: your profile info, tasks you created, tasks assigned to you, comments you posted, and files you uploaded.
4. The export file is available for download within 1 hour (or longer for large accounts).
5. The download link expires after 7 days.

Workspace-level exports (all projects, all tasks) can be done by a workspace Admin under Workspace Settings → Import/Export → Export All Data. This is separate from personal account export.
