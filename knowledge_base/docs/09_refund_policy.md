# TaskFlow Refund Policy

## Monthly Plans

**Full refund within 14 days of purchase.** If you're not satisfied with TaskFlow Pro or Business within the first 14 days of your subscription, request a full refund — no questions asked.

After 14 days, monthly plans are non-refundable for the current billing cycle. You can cancel at any time, and your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged for the next cycle.

**Prorated refunds** are available if you downgrade mid-cycle after the 14-day window. The unused portion of the current month is credited to your TaskFlow account balance, which is applied to future charges. Account balance credits do not expire but are not redeemable for cash.

## Annual Plans

**Full refund within 30 days of purchase.** Annual plans have an extended refund window of 30 days from the date of purchase.

After 30 days, annual plans are non-refundable. If you cancel an annual plan after 30 days, your plan remains active until the end of the annual billing period. No partial refund is issued.

**Important:** If you upgrade from a monthly plan to an annual plan, the 30-day refund window starts from the date of the annual plan purchase, not your original monthly subscription date.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Go to Workspace Settings → Billing → Subscription.
2. Click "Request Refund."
3. Select a reason for the refund (optional but appreciated).
4. Confirm the request.

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days. The exact timing depends on your bank or credit card provider.

Alternatively, email billing@taskflow.io with your workspace name and the email address associated with your account. Our billing team responds within 1 business day.

## Exceptions

- Refunds are not available for workspaces that have been terminated for Terms of Service violations.
- If a payment was made in error (e.g., duplicate charge), contact billing@taskflow.io immediately. Duplicate charges are refunded in full regardless of timing.
- Free plan users are never charged. If you see an unexpected charge, contact billing@taskflow.io.

## Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank instead of requesting a refund through TaskFlow, your workspace will be suspended until the dispute is resolved. We strongly recommend contacting our billing team first — we process refunds faster than most bank dispute processes.
